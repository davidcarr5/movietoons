The Movietoons Process

Our platform is designed to be intuitive and fun, turning a complex animation workflow into a simple, magical experience.

1

Upload Your Memory

Start by uploading your raw footage—photos, video clips, or a mix of both. Movietoons accepts all common formats (MP4, MOV, AVI, WMV, JPEG, PNG, HEIC).

Tell us the story you want to tell. Share key people, dates, and the emotion you want to capture—Joyful Birthday, Nostalgic Vacation, Epic Adventure.

2

Choose Your Style & Soundtrack

Select the vibe that fits your memory. Options include Playful Cartoon, Cinematic Nostalgia, or Pixar-Inspired wonder.

Pick a soundtrack from our curated library of licensed music. Want to provide your own track? Let us know, and we’ll help you use it legally and beautifully.

3

Press Play & Share

Our proprietary engine, guided by human creative direction, transforms your content into a seamless, animated movie.

We deliver your final Movietune in high resolution, ready to download, share, and replay forever.

The technology behind the magic

Movietoons leverages advanced AI for scene analysis and object tracking, allowing us to apply complex animation effects with speed and precision. Every project is still reviewed by human creative directors to ensure it retains the warmth, charm, and emotional integrity of your original memory.

  • AI-assisted animation with handcrafted review.
  • Secure, encrypted storage for every upload.
