### Frequently Asked Questions

#### 5.1. Getting Started with Movietoons

*   **What is Movietoons, in simple terms?**
    Movietoons is a service that transforms your personal photos and videos into short, animated, music-driven movies, giving your memories a magical, cinematic feel.
*   **Do I need editing experience to use Movietoons?**
    Absolutely not! Movietoons is designed to be completely user-friendly. You simply upload your content, choose your style, and we handle the rest of the animation and editing magic.
*   **What kind of memories work best—photos or videos?**
    Both work wonderfully! Videos provide motion for the animation, while photos can be animated and brought to life with our unique styles. A mix of both often creates the most dynamic "memory movie."
*   **Can I use Movietoons from Ireland, the UK, or elsewhere?**
    Yes! Movietoons is a global service. While our founder, David Carr, is from Notre Dame, Ireland, we serve families and creators across Europe, the US, and the entire world.
*   **How long does it take to get my memory movie?**
    Depending on the complexity and current demand, a standard memory movie is typically delivered within 24-48 hours. Expedited options are available for urgent projects.

#### 5.2. Process & Features

*   **How does Movietoons turn my memories into a movie?**
    We use a blend of proprietary animation technology and creative direction to analyze your content, apply a chosen cartoon-inspired style, and synchronize it with a perfect soundtrack, creating a seamless, animated story.
*   **Can I choose different animation or visual styles?**
    Yes, we offer a range of styles, from "Playful Cartoon" to "Cinematic Nostalgia." You can select the style that best fits the emotion of your memory during the upload process.
*   **Can I add my own music, or do you provide tracks?**
    We provide a curated library of licensed, royalty-free music that perfectly complements our animated styles. For legal reasons, we recommend using our library, but you can also provide a track for personal use if you have the necessary rights.
*   **Can I include text, captions, or quotes in my memory movie?**
    Yes, you can specify key text, dates, or quotes during the submission process, and our team will integrate them into the animation in a playful, stylized way.
*   **Can I create more than one version of the same memory?**
    Absolutely! Many users create multiple versions of a single memory using different styles or soundtracks to capture various moods.

#### 5.3. Pricing & Plans

*   **How much does Movietoons cost?**
    We offer tiered pricing based on the length and complexity of your memory movie. Please visit our dedicated Pricing page for a detailed breakdown of our Starter, Pro, and Studio plans.
*   **Do you offer one-off projects or subscriptions?**
    We offer both! You can purchase a single memory movie project, or subscribe to a plan for regular, discounted memory movie creation throughout the year.
*   **Do you offer discounts for families or creators?**
    Yes, our Pro and Studio plans are specifically designed for families and content creators who need to transform memories frequently, offering significant savings over single-project purchases.
*   **Are there extra costs for longer videos or more complex edits?**
    Our pricing tiers clearly outline the maximum length and complexity included. Any requests exceeding the plan limits will be quoted upfront, with no hidden fees.
*   **What’s your refund or satisfaction policy?**
    We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you are not completely delighted with your memory movie, we will work with you to revise it or offer a full refund.

#### 5.4. Privacy, Security & Rights

*   **Who owns the final memory movie—me or Movietoons?**
    You retain full ownership and copyright of your original content. Movietoons grants you a perpetual license to the final memory movie for personal and commercial use (depending on your plan).
*   **How do you store my photos and videos?**
    Your uploaded content is stored securely on encrypted servers and is only accessible by the Movietoons team members working on your project. We delete all source files 30 days after project completion.
*   **Can I delete my data and memory projects at any time?**
    Yes, you have full control. You can request the permanent deletion of your account, source files, and final projects at any time through your user dashboard or by contacting support.
*   **Do you share my content publicly or on social media?**
    We **never** share your content publicly without your explicit, written permission. We may ask to feature your memory movie as an example, but the choice is always yours.
*   **Can I use my memory movie on YouTube, Instagram, or TikTok?**
    Yes, the final memory movie is delivered in a high-quality, shareable format perfect for all social media platforms. The music we use is licensed for this purpose.

#### 5.5. Technical & Support

*   **What file types can I upload?**
    We accept all common video formats (MP4, MOV, AVI, WMV) and image formats (JPEG, PNG, HEIC). If you have an unusual format, please contact us first.
*   **Is there a maximum file size or length?**
    While we recommend keeping individual uploads under 5GB for speed, our platform can handle large files. The maximum final video length depends on your chosen plan.
*   **Can I use Movietoons on mobile and desktop?**
    Yes, our website is fully responsive, allowing you to upload content and manage your projects seamlessly from any mobile device, tablet, or desktop computer.
*   **What should I do if something goes wrong with my upload?**
    If you encounter any issues, please try refreshing the page. If the problem persists, contact our support team immediately via the Contact page, and we will assist you with an alternative upload method.
*   **How do I contact support if I’m in a different time zone?**
    Our support team operates globally. You can submit a ticket via our Contact page 24/7, and we guarantee a response within 12 hours, regardless of your time zone.
