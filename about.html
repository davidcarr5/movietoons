Our Mission: To animate your life

Every moment deserves the emotional power of an animated film. Movietoons turns your personal videos and photos into music-driven stories that capture nostalgia, creativity, warmth, and magic.

Our Values

  • Nostalgia: We honor the past, believing that memories are the most valuable stories we possess.
  • Creativity: We push the boundaries of what a memory can be, turning raw footage into art.
  • Warmth & Charm: Our brand is friendly, accessible, and built on joy.
  • Magic: We make every experience feel special, whimsical, and cinematic.
Illustration: David Carr with childhood movie memories swirling around.

The Founder’s Journey

The story of Movietoons is deeply intertwined with the passion of its founder, David Carr.

David grew up in Notre Dame, Ireland, with a lifelong fascination for animated storytelling, especially the works of Pixar. After founding Pi-Squared—a venture dedicated to creative technology—David set out to bring animation magic to our most personal stories.

Movietoons is the realization of that dream: a platform that combines cinematic visuals, music, and heartfelt narratives to transform memories into movies.
