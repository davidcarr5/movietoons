## The Movietoons Process: Memory to Movie in 3 Steps

Our platform is designed to be intuitive and fun, turning a complex animation process into a simple, magical experience for you.

### Step 1: Upload Your Memory

*   **What to Upload:** Start by uploading your raw footageâ€”photos, video clips, or a mix of both. Our system accepts all common file formats.
*   **Tell Us the Story:** Provide a brief description of the memory, the key people involved, and the emotion you want to capture (e.g., Joyful Birthday, Nostalgic Vacation, Epic Adventure).

### Step 2: Choose Your Style & Soundtrack

*   **Select Your Vibe:** Browse our library of animation styles. Options include:
    *   **Playful Cartoon:** Bright colors, bouncy motion, thick outlines.
    *   **Cinematic Nostalgia:** Warm tones, film grain, gentle transitions.
    *   **Pixar-Inspired:** Soft shading, expressive characters, emotional focus.
*   **Pick Your Music:** Select a track from our licensed library that perfectly matches the mood of your memory.

### Step 3: Press Play & Share

*   **Transformation:** Our proprietary engine, guided by human creative direction, transforms your content into a seamless, animated movie.
*   **Delivery:** We deliver your final Movietune in high-resolution, ready to download and share on social media, with family, or keep forever.
